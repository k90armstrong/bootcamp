Blind Pilot

About

The band, Blind Pilot, is based in Portland, Oregon. They play music in the American indie folk genre. They have released three albums:

Album Year
3 Rounds and a Sound 2008
We Are the Tide 2011
And Then Like Lions 2016

Band Members

  • Isreal Nebeker - Vocals, Guitar
  • Ryan Dobrowski - Drums, Percusion
  • Luke Ydstie - Upright Bass, Bass Guitar, Backing Vocals
  • Kati Clabborn - Banjo, Dulcimer, Ukulele, Guitar, Backing Vocals
  • Dave Jorgesen - Trumpet, Keyboard
  • Ian Krist - Vibraphone, Percusion

Bike Tour

In 2008, Blind Pilot did a very unique tour along the West Coast. Taking nothing but, their bikes, bike trailers, instruments and some necessities, not knowing where they would sleep at night, they headed down the coast playing at many small venues. The band actually decided on the name "Blind Pilot" before leaving on the tour because they had not heard of anyone doing it before and felt as if they were flying into the unknown. At the end of the tour, they recorded the album "3 Rounds and a Sound".
